Cloud & Infrastructure Services (CIS)

Flexible solutions for your Hiring and Recruitment needs

Spyke Infotech is a leading provider of cloud and virtualization technologies, helping enterprises enhance their digital experience and drive business growth. We envision modern IT data centers equipped with secure and service-oriented infrastructure services. Our goal is to assist customers in adopting the best hybrid data center management architecture for improved agility. Our data center management services, which include operations, transformation, automation, and cloud services, have helped more than 100 enterprises successfully migrate to global data centers. Currently, we manage over half a million servers globally.

Our customer-focused Next-Generation Data Center (NGDC) suite leverages disruptive technology, flexible commercial models, and global delivery models to deliver value to business, IT, and end users. This enables businesses to build competency in providing data center and cloud migration services worldwide and adopt a future-proof hybrid cloud infrastructure.

End users benefit from improved agility and more automated and responsive business services with our data center management solutions. Our offerings, including MCOD, RecoverNXT CLOUD, BackupNXT, ROBOace, CART, MyCloud, DRYiCETM, and SDI Innovation Garage, help IT reduce the total cost of ownership and enhance operational excellence. Our strong automation and process frameworks ensure efficient and verified reference data center architecture-based solutions for end users.
